## Steps

1. Cook onions with olive oil and a bit of sugar until soft/translucent (~10 min).
2. Add grated tomatoes, salt, pepper (+ a bit of water if needed). Cook a few minutes.
3. Add green beans + carrots + red peppers. Stir to coat.
4. Cook until tender (pressure cooker noted as ~17 minutes).

## Notes

- Traditionally eaten cold.
