6-8 zucchinis.
Filling: 500gr ground beef/chicken/turkey, 2 tbsp rice (or bulgur), onion grated, parsley, pepper, olive oil, 2-3 grated tomatoes.
Core zucchini, arrange scraps bottom, stuff, plate on top. Add water/tomato water. Boil then medium 40-50 min.
