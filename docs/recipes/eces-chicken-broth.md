## Steps

1. Rinse chicken under cold water.
2. Add chicken to a large pot with cold water (enough to cover by ~2.5 cm).
3. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a low simmer for ~30 minutes. Skim scum.
4. Add onion, ginger, garlic, green onions, and carrots.
5. Simmer uncovered on low heat for ~3 hours.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Strain. (Optional: reserve some meat/veg + broth as chunky soup.)

## Notes

- Fridge: up to 5 days.
- Freezer: up to 3 months.
- If it gels in the fridge, that’s normal (gelatin). It liquifies when heated.

### Freezing in glass jars

Fill jars leaving ~5 cm headspace. Freeze with lids loosely on; tighten after frozen.
