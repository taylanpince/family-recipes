## Steps

1. Soak beans overnight.
2. Pressure cook beans 5 min, discard the dark water. Set beans aside.
3. In the cooker (lid off), sauté onion + carrot + garlic + red pepper in olive oil briefly.
4. Add grated tomatoes, brown sugar, salt, and a bit of water/broth.
5. Add beans back in. Pressure cook ~15 min.

## Notes

- Source note: typically eaten cold.
- Fresh beans variation was noted in source; we can add it later if needed.
