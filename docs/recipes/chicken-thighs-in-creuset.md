  - 10–12 boneless chicken thighs
  - 1 onion, sliced into large circles
  - 2 tbsp brown sugar
  - 2 tbsp paprika
  - 1 clove garlic, crushed
  - juice of 1/2 lemon
  - olive oil
  - salt
  - black pepper
---

## Steps

1. Make marinade: brown sugar + paprika + salt + pepper + olive oil + crushed garlic + lemon juice.
2. Coat chicken thighs and marinate in the fridge ~30 minutes.
3. In a Dutch oven (Creuset), add onion slices + a bit of olive oil to cover the bottom.
4. Add chicken plus all marinade.
5. Cook gently (low/medium-low) for **1.5 hours+** until tender and falling apart.

## Notes

- If it starts to dry out, add a splash of water.
