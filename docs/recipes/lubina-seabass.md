## Recipe details

- **Difficulty:** easy
- **Total time:** 50 min
- **Servings:** 4
- **Tags:** fish, oven, weeknight

## Ingredients

- 1–2 whole seabass (lubina), ~1.2–1.5 kg each
- olive oil
- red wine vinegar or sherry vinegar
- 2 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- salt

<!-- GENERATED_RECIPE_METADATA_END -->

![Lubina (Seabass)](../assets/recipes/lubina-seabass.svg)

## Steps

1. Rinse/clean the fish with fresh water.
2. Coat with olive oil and red wine vinegar or sherry vinegar.
3. Thinly slice garlic and place slices inside and outside the fish.
4. Salt the top of the fish.
5. Heat oven to **225°C**.
6. Roast **35–40 minutes**.

## Notes

- If you want, we can add a "doneness check" (thickest part flakes easily; internal ~55–60°C).
