## Steps

1. Sear beef cubes in olive oil over medium-high heat (~5 min).
2. Add grated onion and chopped carrots; cook until slightly caramelized.
3. Add grated tomatoes. Add stock/water if needed.
4. Pressure cook ~30 min on low/steady heat.
5. Open the cooker. Add peas (and potatoes if using).
6. Cook ~15 min more over medium heat.

## Notes

- Adjust liquids depending on how saucy you want it.
