  - 1 red pepper, diced
  - 2–3 tomatoes, grated
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste

  - black pepper
  - water (enough to cover)

![White Beans with Beef](../assets/recipes/white-beans-with-beef.svg)

## Steps

1. Soak beans overnight.
2. In a pressure cooker, heat olive oil and sauté onion + garlic until fragrant.
3. Add beef cubes and brown on all sides.
4. Add red pepper; cook a few minutes until slightly softened.
5. Add grated tomatoes and tomato paste; stir.
6. Add drained beans. Season with salt + pepper.
7. Add enough water to cover.
8. Pressure cook ~45 minutes (adjust to your cooker / bean age).

## Notes

- If you prefer a thinner stew, add more water/stock after cooking and simmer uncovered.
