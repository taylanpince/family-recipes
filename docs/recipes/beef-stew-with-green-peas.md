Sear beef cubes in olive oil (med-high, 5 min)
Add grated onions + chopped carrots; caramelize.
Add grated tomatoes (+ stock/water as needed)
Pressure cook 30 min low.
Open, add peas + potatoes; cook 15 min medium.
