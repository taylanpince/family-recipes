Soak overnight (2 pints)
Pressure cook 5 min; discard black water.

Sauté: 1 grated onion, 1 chopped carrot, 1 diced garlic, 1 chopped red pepper.
Add: 4 grated tomatoes, 1 tsp brown sugar, tiny salt, bit water/broth.
Pressure cook 15 min.
