ingredients:
  - 2 cups red lentils (wash + soak ~30 min)
  - 2 onions, diced
  - 2–3 carrots, diced
  - 2 tbsp flour
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 2 potatoes, boiled until tender
  - 1 celery stalk, diced very small
  - 2 cups meat stock
  - 1 tsp ground cumin
  - olive oil
  - water (enough to cover)
  - salt
  - black pepper
---

## Steps

1. Cook onions in olive oil on medium-low until tender.
2. Add carrots and continue cooking.
3. Add drained lentils; stir.
4. Add flour; stir until incorporated.
5. Add tomato paste; keep stirring.
6. Add boiled potatoes.
7. Add enough water to cover.
8. Bring to a bubble, then reduce to low and cook ~1 hour.
9. Blend smooth with an immersion blender.
10. Add meat stock and cumin; stir.
11. Add diced celery.
12. Bring back to a brief bubble (~1 min). Adjust salt/cumin.

## Notes

- You can thin with extra stock/water after blending.
