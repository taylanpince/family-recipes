## Recipe details

- **Cuisine:** Turkish
- **Difficulty:** easy
- **Total time:** 80 min
- **Servings:** 6
- **Tags:** stew, turkish, family

## Ingredients

- green beans
- 500 g minced meat
- 1 onion, minced
- 2 sweet red peppers, diced small
- 2 carrots, diced
- 3 tomatoes, grated
- olive oil
- salt
- black pepper

<!-- GENERATED_RECIPE_METADATA_END -->

![Green Beans with Minced Meat](../assets/recipes/green-beans-with-minced-meat.svg)

## Steps

1. Heat olive oil and cook onion until tender.
2. Add peppers and carrots; cook on medium-low until slightly softened.
3. Add minced meat; cook until browned.
4. Add green beans; cook a few minutes.
5. Add grated tomatoes.
6. Cover and cook ~1 hour (normal pot).

## Notes

- Add a splash of water if it gets too dry while simmering.
