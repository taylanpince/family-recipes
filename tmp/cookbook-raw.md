## blueberry pancakes

1 egg
1 cup flour
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons melted butter
1/2 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder

mix all

grease heated pan if necessary (to test sprinkle a few drops of water)

pour 3 tablespoons of batter

variations:
- drop 2-3 blueberries while cooking
- or raisins + walnuts + cinnamon

# blueberry pancakes (version 2)

1 egg
1 cup flour
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons melted butter
1/2 tablespoon sugar
1 pack baking powder (~1 tsp)

- squeeze a bit of lemon in the baking powder
- 1 pinch of salt

mix all

grease heated pan if necessary
pour 3 tablespoons of batter

+1 cup blueberries
=9 pancakes

# chicken thighs in creuset

10-12 boneless medium chicken thighs

Marinade: 2 tbsp brown sugar, 2 tbsp paprika, pinch salt, black pepper, olive oil, 1 crushed garlic clove, juice of 1/2 lemon. Marinate 30 min.

Cook: 1 onion in large circles at bottom with olive oil. Add chicken + marinade. Cook 1.5h+ until tender.

# green beans with minced meat

- 500gr minced meat
- 1 onion minced
- 2 sweet red peppers diced small
- 2 carrots diced
- 3 tomatoes grated

Cook onions → add peppers/carrots → add meat brown → add green beans → add tomatoes → cover and cook 1h.

# barbun (pinto) beans (cold summer dish)

Soak overnight (2 pints)
Pressure cook 5 min; discard black water.

Sauté: 1 grated onion, 1 chopped carrot, 1 diced garlic, 1 chopped red pepper.
Add: 4 grated tomatoes, 1 tsp brown sugar, tiny salt, bit water/broth.
Pressure cook 15 min.

# beef stew with green peas

Sear beef cubes in olive oil (med-high, 5 min)
Add grated onions + chopped carrots; caramelize.
Add grated tomatoes (+ stock/water as needed)
Pressure cook 30 min low.
Open, add peas + potatoes; cook 15 min medium.

# swiss chard dolma

500 gr ground beef, 2-3 tomatoes grated, bulgur (1/2 pint glass), onion grated, black pepper.
Wrap leaves, pack tightly. Roots on bottom, bit water. Low heat 1h.

# zucchini dolma

6-8 zucchinis.
Filling: 500gr ground beef/chicken/turkey, 2 tbsp rice (or bulgur), onion grated, parsley, pepper, olive oil, 2-3 grated tomatoes.
Core zucchini, arrange scraps bottom, stuff, plate on top. Add water/tomato water. Boil then medium 40-50 min.

# vine leaf dolma

500gr ground beef, 2 tbsp rice, onion grated, parsley, pepper, olive oil, 2 big tomatoes grated.
Dip leaves 30s in hot water.
Cook with ~1 cup water, plate on top; boil then low.

# black eyed peas salad (cold dish)

500gr.
Boil, discard first black water. Reboil with salt. Leave a bit of water in pot.
DO NOT add onions yet.
Finish with lots parsley, 4-5 green onions, lots olive oil, apple vinegar.

# chickpeas with beef

Soak 1 pint glass chickpeas overnight.
Sear 500gr meat cubes; add onion + diced red pepper + carrot.
Add chickpeas + lots grated tomatoes (cover meat) + broth if needed.
Pressure cook 1h.

# white beans with beef

Soak 1 pint glass beans overnight.
1 onion diced, 2 garlic diced, 1 red pepper diced, 2-3 tomatoes grated, 1 tbsp tomato paste, 500gr meat cubes.
Steps: sauté onion/garlic → add meat brown → add pepper → add tomatoes → add beans + paste → cover with water; pressure cook 45 min.

# kisir (turkish)

2 cups fine bulgur, onion, green onions, parsley, dill, mint, tomatoes, bell peppers, cucumber.
Lemon, paprika, pepper, cumin, olive oil (~1/2 cup), pepper-tomato mix, tomato paste.
Pour boiling water over bulgur. Cook onion a bit, add paste, off heat. Mix, knead with herbs. Add oil, salt, lemon.
