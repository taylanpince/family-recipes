## Recipe details

- **Difficulty:** medium
- **Total time:** 150 min
- **Servings:** 4
- **Tags:** lamb, creuset, roast

## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg lamb sirloin, cut into 8 pieces
- Lots of garlic
- Fresh rosemary
- Bay leaves
- Baby potatoes
- 2 onions, quartered
- Beef stock
- Half a bottle of red wine
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper

<!-- GENERATED_RECIPE_METADATA_END -->

## Steps

1. Season lamb pieces with salt and pepper.
2. Sear lamb in the Creuset on high heat until browned on all sides. Remove and set aside.
3. Add garlic, rosemary, bay leaves, onions, and baby potatoes to the pot.
4. Add beef stock and half a bottle of red wine.
5. Return lamb to the pot.
6. Cook covered at 150°C (300°F) for 2 hours until very tender.

## Notes

- Inspired by RecipeTin Eats Greek slow-roasted lamb.
