## Recipe details

- **Cuisine:** Turkish
- **Difficulty:** medium
- **Total time:** 70 min
- **Servings:** 4
- **Tags:** turkish, stuffed, family

## Ingredients

- 6–8 zucchinis
- 500 g ground beef (or chicken/turkey)
- 2 tbsp rice (or bulgur)
- 1 onion, finely grated
- parsley, finely chopped
- 2–3 tomatoes, grated
- olive oil
- black pepper
- salt
- water (or tomato-water mix for cooking)

<!-- GENERATED_RECIPE_METADATA_END -->

![Zucchini Dolma](../assets/recipes/zucchini-dolma.svg)

## Steps

1. Cut ends off zucchini, cut in half, and core (leave ~1 inch at the bottom).
2. Mix filling: ground meat + onion + rice/bulgur + parsley + salt/pepper + (optionally) some grated tomato.
3. Put scooped zucchini flesh around the edges of the pot.
4. Stuff zucchini and arrange tightly in the center.
5. If you have extra filling, form small patties/balls and place on top.
6. Put a plate on top to hold everything in place.
7. Add a bit of water / grated-tomato-water mix so it won’t burn.
8. Bring to a boil, then cook ~40–50 minutes at medium heat.

## Notes

- Keep the pot fairly snug so the dolma holds together.
