ingredients:
  - ½ block (≈ 1 cup) butter, room temperature
  - 1 cup yogurt
  - ½ cup olive oil
  - 2 egg whites
  - 2 egg yolks
  - 1 tsp salt
  - 3 cups flour
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - ½ cup fresh dill, finely chopped
  - 1 block white cheese (beyaz peynir / feta), grated
  - Nigella seeds (çörekotu), for topping
---

## Steps

1. Leave butter at room temperature for 1 hour until very soft.
2. In a large glass bowl, knead and soften the butter.
3. Add yogurt, olive oil, egg whites, and salt. Mix well.
4. Gradually add flour and baking powder, kneading until the dough comes together and no longer sticks to your hands.
5. Place a damp paper towel over the dough and rest in the fridge for 30 minutes.
6. Meanwhile, mix egg yolks with a little olive oil (for the egg wash). Finely chop dill and grate the white cheese.
7. Shape the poğaças by hand, filling each with dill and grated cheese.
8. Brush tops with egg wash and sprinkle with nigella seeds.
9. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 40–45 minutes until golden.

## Notes

- Anneanne's recipe. Handle the dough gently — don't overwork it after the flour goes in.
- Beyaz peynir can be substituted with feta, though it's saltier — adjust salt accordingly.
