## Recipe details

- **Cuisine:** Turkish
- **Difficulty:** easy
- **Total time:** 45 min
- **Servings:** 6
- **Tags:** soup, turkish, pressure-cooker

## Ingredients

- 1.5 pint glasses red lentils
- 1 carrot
- 1 big onion
- 1–2 cloves garlic
- 2 tomatoes
- 1 leek (optional)
- water (about 6 soup bowls)
- salt

<!-- GENERATED_RECIPE_METADATA_END -->

![Red Lentil Soup](../assets/recipes/red-lentil-soup.svg)

## Steps

1. Add ingredients to pressure cooker with water.
2. Pressure cook ~30 minutes.
3. Blend if you want it smooth; season to taste.
