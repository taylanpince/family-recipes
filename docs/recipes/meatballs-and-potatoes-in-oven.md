## Steps

1. Toss potatoes with olive oil in a baking tray.
2. Place meatballs on top of potatoes.
3. Add thick tomato slices and red peppers.
4. Add a splash of water.
5. Bake at **180°C** for ~**30 minutes** (adjust as needed).
