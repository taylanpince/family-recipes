## Steps

1. Scrub beets thoroughly.
2. Boil in enough water to cover for 30–40 minutes until tender.
3. Peel the beets and let them cool.
4. Make brine: boil water with 2 tbsp salt, then let cool completely.
5. Once beets are cool, slice into apple-wedge shapes.
6. Layer beets and garlic alternately in a jar.
7. Mix the beet cooking liquid with the cooled salt brine, add 1 tsp sugar.
8. Pour into the jar, leaving a little space at the top.
9. Add grape vinegar (about one coffee cup / 60ml).
10. Leave in a cool, shaded spot for at least 2 days before eating.

## Notes

- time_total_min reflects 2 days of pickling time.
- The beet cooking liquid gives it a beautiful deep red colour.
- Great served cold as a side or meze.
