1 egg
1 cup flour
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons melted butter
1/2 tablespoon sugar
1 pack baking powder (~1 tsp)

- squeeze a bit of lemon in the baking powder
- 1 pinch of salt

mix all

grease heated pan if necessary
pour 3 tablespoons of batter

+1 cup blueberries
=9 pancakes
